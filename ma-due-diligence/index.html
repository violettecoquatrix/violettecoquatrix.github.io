The Gap

Management presentations tell a story. The shop floor tells a different one.

Your financial model assumes margins hold, systems scale, and the team can execute a post-close plan. But nobody stress-tested those assumptions against how decisions actually get made inside this business. Nobody checked how dependent the supply chain is on two vendors who haven't been renegotiated in four years.
